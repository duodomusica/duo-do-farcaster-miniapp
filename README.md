# 🎵 Dúo Dø at FarCon Rome — Farcaster Mini App
 
> *Genre-free and in-motion music. A 45-minute onchain acoustic performance premiering at FarCon Rome.*
 
---
 
## 🌍 What is this?
 
**Dúo Dø at FarCon Rome** is a Farcaster Mini App that lets the Farcaster community become part of something bigger: the funding and creation of a new original song, recorded and performed live at **FarCon Rome**, during the **Kismet Casa residency**.
 
The app connects music, onchain culture, and real community participation — turning supporters into Executive Producers with exclusive rewards.
 
---
 
## ✨ Features
 
- 🎯 **Fundraising progress bar** — See in real time how the community is reaching the goal together
- 🏆 **Executive Producer Leaderboard** — Recognize and celebrate every contributor
- 💸 **USDC donations on Base** — Simple, fast, onchain contributions
- 🎧 **Listen to our music** — Direct link to our Tortoise mini app (another Farcaster native experience)
- 📖 **Learn more about the project** — Discover the story behind Dúo Dø and our artistic vision
- 📬 **Contact us** — Send us a message directly from the app

## 🎬 The Story
 
**Dúo Dø** is an Argentine musical duo with over a decade of international performances. Genre-free and always in motion, we create music that takes audiences on a journey of sensations.
 
For **FarCon Rome**, we're participating in the **Kismet Casa residency** — an artists-in-residence program that runs alongside one of the most important Farcaster community events in Europe. During this residency, we'll compose and record a brand new original song in collaboration with **Tortoise** (a Farcaster-native music app), and perform it live at FarCon Rome.
 
This mini app is our way of bringing the Farcaster community into the creative process — not just as an audience, but as **co-creators and Executive Producers**.
